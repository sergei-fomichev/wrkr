Welcome to WoRKeR

Wrkr is a collaborative effort between a user's smart phone, smart watch, and PC with Leap Motion connected with our website to help prevent a vast majority of onset wrist problems such as arthritis.

The goal of this application is to track when an office worker is at a keyboard, and after spending a set amount of time on their computer, be prompted to exercise their wrists. Our website will provide the user specialized exercises to execute above his/her Leap Motion device with feedback as to how (s)he is doing.
